Tom Holland


Birth

He was born on June 1st 1996, in Kingston upon Thames, London.


Early Life

Started taking Dance lessons at the age of 10 and practiced gymnastics before that, laying a foundation for his future in performing.


Start in Performing

Tom Holland started out acting in local musicals with his family and shortly after graduated to the big screen.


Tom in Film

Landing a leading role in the animated movie, Arietty as Sho and a part in the movie that would catch Marvel's eye, The Impossible.


Spider-Tom

Now Tom Holland is famous, fulfilling his dream of playing his childhood hero while never losing who he is.


Tom Holland as a Celebrity

From modeling for GQ, to beating Zendaya on Lip Sync Battle to interviewing with seemingly everyone ,even while distracted by puppies, Tom Holland has taken the world by storm, even gaining a growing fanbase.
